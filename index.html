Pathfinding: Waldo's Journey Home

Tooltips

Search

Background:

After travelling the world and seeing many things of great intrigue, Waldo has decided to return home to visit his family and his puppy Wilhelm III.

He is consulting his handy pocket map and trying to determine the optimal path from his current position to his home. As he has a degree is mathematics, he decides to do this considering one of the search algorithms he is familiar with.

Instructions:

Draw out a map of the terrain to conduct pathfinding in. Different tooltips will place different terrain onto the map; each tooltip has mouseover text describing what it places. When you are satisfied with your map place the start and end markers, select your search, and start.

Tooltips:

Start Marker

Places your start marker. This must be placed to run a search.

Finish Marker

Places your finish marker. This must be placed to run a search.

Water

Places a Water Tile. Water tiles cannot be passed through.

Road

Places a Road Tile. Movement on roads is five times (5x) easier than default.

Forest

Places a Forest Tile. Movement in forests is five times (5x) harder than default.

Eraser

Cleares any tiles from a position. Right-click is always bound to this tooltip.
